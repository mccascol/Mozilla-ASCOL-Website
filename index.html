June

  • 24

    Introduction to Common Voice and DeepSpeech

    This is an introductory event about Common Voice and DeepSpeech project of Mozilla in Amrit Campus. In the event, we also discussed about different methods through which we can collect Nepali sentences for Common Voice project. So, as a initiative we made a Telegram Group where we talk only on Nepali language and those chat messages will be fetched and collected by a telegram bot. Later those sentences will be reviewed and submitted for Common Voice Project and others can also use them on their project.


    https://voice.mozilla.org

About Us

Mozilla’s mission is to ensure the Internet is a global public resource, open and accessible to all. A Mozilla University & College Club is a group of students with a passion for technology who meet regularly to advance this mission by building and innovating on open source projects that keep the web open.
